Hi, I am Madhav Bhatia.

7+ years turning data into product & business impact

I started my career as a Data Analyst at LatentView Analytics, where over nearly 3 years I built a deep analytical foundation — mastering clickstream analysis, search engine optimization data, and ETL engineering. By the end, I'd been promoted to Senior Data Analyst and led a team that achieved a 60% reduction in ETL processing time through automation and query optimization.

That data engineering fluency became the launchpad for my next move: product. At OYO, I spent 2+ years as a Product Analyst → Assistant Manager of Product, owning the analytics for the entire booking journey. I led a team of analysts, launched 4 products that drove millions in incremental revenue, ran A/B tests that measurably improved app conversion rates, and built the fraud detection algorithms that protected the platform.

Wanting to formalize the intersection of data science and business strategy, I earned a Master of Science in Business Analytics from the University at Buffalo, SUNY — graduating with a perfect 4.0 GPA. During my program, I also consulted for local organizations through UB's Project Clinic, turning academic knowledge into real-world impact.

Today, I'm back at LatentView Analytics as a Customer Insights & Analytics professional — bringing the full loop of data engineering, product ownership, and business analytics to enterprise clients. I hold certifications in Microsoft Fabric, Azure Data Science, Power BI, and am a Certified ScrumMaster®. I'm also co-inventor of a patented IoT-based energy monitoring system (issued 2023).
